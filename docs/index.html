How It Works

1

Enable Forza Data Out

In FH6 settings, enable "Data Out" on UDP port 5300. This broadcasts live telemetry to AI Tuner.

2

Launch AI Tuner

Run the app, enter your free Gemini API key in Settings. The overlay appears and connects to telemetry automatically.

3

Drive & Identify

Start driving. The engineer detects your car and asks you to confirm the name and discipline. Quick-select buttons make it instant.

4

Collect & Analyze

Drive for a few minutes while the engineer builds a telemetry profile — tire temps, balance, slip patterns, G-forces.

5

Get Tuning Advice

Describe what feels wrong or just ask. The engineer cross-references your telemetry data and suggests relative adjustments. Apply them in the garage and keep driving.
